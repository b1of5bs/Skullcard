Pirate Abilities




Rosie D'laney

Pick who leads next, including you.


Rascal of Rotan

Bid 0, 10, or 20 points. Add them if you win your bid, subtract them if you lose your bid.


Bendt the Bandit

Draw two from the draw pile, then discard two from your hand.


Juanita Jade

View the undealt cards to see what's not in play.


Harry the Giant

Adjust your bid by +1, -1, or leave it alone.


The Tigress

Either Escape or Pirate. Declare when played.

These abilities may not be used during the final trick of a round (except for Tigress and Harry the Giant) and don't carry to the next round.
