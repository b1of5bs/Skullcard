Card Hierarchy

The Skull King

Defeats all cards except the Mermaids.

Pirates

Defeat all cards except the Skull King.

Mermaids

Defeat all numbered cards and the Skull King.

Trump Suit

Defeats all the standard suits. Highest trump card wins.

Standard Suits

High number wins of suit that was lead.

Escapes

Lose to all cards, even previously played escapes.
